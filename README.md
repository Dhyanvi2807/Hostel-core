 HostelCareHostelCare is a simple website I built to help students in hostels report daily issues without the headache of finding a warden or writing in a physical register. The ProblemIn most hostels, reporting a problem is a mess:Staff forgets: If I tell a guard my light is out, they often forget the room number.No proof: There’s no written record of the complaint.Waiting game: Students never know if someone is actually coming to fix the issue. My SolutionI made a digital platform that's fast and easy:Tap & Report: Just click a category like Food, Maintenance, or Cleaning.No Confusion: The system keeps track of the room number and the exact problem.Live Updates: I included a system to see if a complaint is "Pending" or "Resolved" immediately.Simple Design: I used icons so it’s easy to use even if a student is in a rush or faces a language barrier.Tech I UsedHTML & CSS: To build the pages and make them look good.Bootstrap: To make sure it works perfectly on mobile phones.JavaScript: To make the buttons and navigation work.User Research: I studied the daily struggles of students and staff to find out their biggest annoyances
